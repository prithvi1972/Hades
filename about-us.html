- Who Am I -

An Electronics and Telecommunication engineer who soon found out that the business of communicating ideas is more electrifying than sitting in a cubicle repairing TV sets.

In long stints at Mudra Communications and McCann Erickson India as a Creative Director, worked closely with the advertising legends, Freddy Birdy and Naved Akhtar.

Went to head the creative department of Rediffusion-Young & Rubicam at Bangalore and won the coveted agency of the year twice in succession. Received many national and international awards including the Copywriter of the Year.

I believe that love is the greatest reward. The love of clients, colleagues and consumers powers my creativity... And my love for life has made me a spiritual person.

- What I Do? -

Quite early in my advertising career I started directing ad films. Presently, I am writing feature films for Bollywood and preparing for my directorial debut with a grand Bollywood-Hollywood production.
